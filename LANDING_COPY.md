## FAQ

### 1. Is this really free?
**Yes.** The audit tool is completely free with no credit card required. We make money by helping companies with significant savings opportunities ($500+/month) access discounted AI credits through Credex's marketplace.

### 2. Do I need to sign up or create an account?
**No.** You can run an audit and see results without any signup. We only ask for your email if you want to save the report or book a consultation for high-savings cases.

### 3. How accurate are the savings estimates?
**Very accurate.** Every recommendation is based on current vendor pricing (verified weekly) and deterministic rules. We show you the math: "4 inactive seats × $30/seat × 12 months = $1,440/year." No black-box AI estimates.

### 4. What if my team is already optimized?
**We'll tell you.** If your audit shows less than $100/month in potential savings, we'll celebrate that — you're spending efficiently. We don't manufacture fake savings to make the tool look good.

### 5. How do you define "inactive" or "underutilized"?
**Transparently.** An inactive seat is one with zero logins in 30 days OR fewer than 5 prompts/requests in 30 days. These thresholds are conservative to avoid false positives. You can see the exact criteria in every recommendation.

### 6. Will this work for my company size?
**Yes, if you have 3+ AI tools.** The tool is most valuable for teams of 10-200 people with multiple AI subscriptions. Smaller teams may not have enough spend to optimize. Enterprise teams (500+) should contact us for a custom analysis.

### 7. What happens to my data?
**It stays private.** Your audit data is stored securely in our database (Supabase with row-level security). The shareable report URL only shows aggregated savings and recommendations — no raw usage data or company details. You can request deletion anytime.

### 8. Can I export the report?
**Yes.** Every audit can be shared via a public URL. PDF export is coming soon. For now, you can print the web page or screenshot the results.

### 9. Do you integrate with our billing systems?
**Not yet.** Currently, you manually input your AI tool spend. Direct integrations with Stripe, AWS billing, and vendor APIs are on the roadmap for Q2 2024.

### 10. What if I disagree with a recommendation?
**That's fine.** Every recommendation includes a confidence score (0-100%) and explicit reasoning. If a recommendation doesn't fit your use case, ignore it. The tool is advisory, not prescriptive.

## Notes on Tone

**What works:**
- Direct and specific ("$4,800/year" not "significant savings")
- Honest about limitations ("Not yet" not "Coming soon")
- Celebrates already-optimized users (don't manufacture fake savings)
- Shows the math (transparency builds trust)

**What doesn't work:**
- Vague promises ("Optimize your workflow")
- Overhyping ("Revolutionary AI-powered platform")
- Hiding the business model (be upfront about Credex consultation for high-savings cases)
- Jargon ("Leverage synergies across your AI stack")

**Target audience:**
- Technical founders and engineering managers
- Cost-conscious but not cheap
- Want data, not sales pitches
- Will forward the report to finance/board

**Key insight from user interviews:**
The tool isn't primarily about saving money — it's about answering stakeholder questions with confidence. The shareable report is the product.
